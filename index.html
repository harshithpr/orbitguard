Case study

2022 Starlink Storm Incident

Kp ~6

SpaceX launched 49 Starlink satellites into a very low deployment orbit near 210 km on February 3, 2022. A geomagnetic storm increased atmospheric drag, and 38 satellites reentered instead of raising to operational altitude. OrbitGuard uses this as a permanent reminder that space weather is mission-critical, not decorative.

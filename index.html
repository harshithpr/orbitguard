I am deeply interested in aerospace engineering and excited to explore the field through hands-on engineering, design, manufacturing, and future work with aerospace companies such as Boeing, Lockheed Martin, and other organizations building the next generation of flight and space systems.

My engineering path started through James Clemens High School, where I began formal engineering coursework in 2024 and took PLTW classes. After moving from Tucson to Texas and then to Madison, I spent significant time studying mathematics and advanced coursework, which helped me connect engineering with the combination of math, design, modeling, and problem solving that I enjoy most.

In my first engineering course, Introduction to Engineering Design, I earned a certification connected to 3D modeling, engineering drawing, and the fundamentals of turning ideas into precise models. During my sophomore year in 2025, I also earned a Python credential after studying programming fundamentals and more advanced topics.
